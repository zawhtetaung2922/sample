Keep Myanmar's Forests

What we are doing to the forests of the world is but a mirror reflection of what we are doing to ourselves and to one another.

Mahatma Gandhi

      As long centuries of years ago, Myanmar has the highest proportion of forest cover in mainland Southeast Asia and some of the region's most intact forests.They also contain exceptional biodiversity, including fishing cats, sun bears, dholes, binturongs, pangolins, and more than 1000 bird species. Myanmar's forests are also home to 70 types of commerically vlauable tree, inculding varities of ironwood and rosewood. But, the species that makes Myanmar famous-- shaping the country's history and driving its economy. According to a report by the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations, Myanmar lost 19% or 7,445,000 hectares (28,750 sa mi) of forest between 1990 and 2010. After this years, Myanmar's deforestation is increasing. Myanamr is ranked third in the courtries with the highest deforestation rates after Brazil and Indonesia. As the results of deforestation, Myanmar faces the flooding in every years. Changing the condition of climate in Myanmar is one of the major issues as the deforestation in Myanmar. A lot of tree are lossing without benefits. So, the environment can be toxic. Finally, deforestation can effect the healthy of Myanmar's people. In Agriculture, farmers can face about the lack of water for their farms. So, it can also effect the business of Myanmar. We must to keep the Myanmar's forests. It is very important and done first major issue by the Government. So, Government and others associations are doing as major issue about reforestation in Myanmar.
